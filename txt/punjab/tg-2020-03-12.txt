Health Medical and Family Welfare Departnnent,
Media Bulletin on Covid-19 Date 12.03.2020
WHO has declared the recent Novel Corona Virus (COVID-19) epidemic affecting 113 countries as International Public Health Emergency of International concern (PHEIC). In this context, Government of Telangana has strengthened the surveillance and control measures against the disease, as per the national guidelines.
>	It	is	advised	that mass	gathering	may be	avoided	or	possibly	be
postponed till the disease spread is contained.
> Universal screening is done to each passenger with history of foreign travel.
>	State	level	committee	meeting	held	at	Secretariat	and	reviewed	the
action taken
>	Passengers	coming	from	abroad	are	requested	to	cooperate	and
answer the calls from 104 HELPLINE.
