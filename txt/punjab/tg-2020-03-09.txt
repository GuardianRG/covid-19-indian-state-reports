Media Bulletin on Covid-19 Date 09.03.2020
WHO has declared the recent Novel Corona Virus (COVID-19) epidemic affecting 102 countries as International Public Health Emergency of International concern (PHEIC). In this context, Government of Telangana has strengthened the surveillance and control measures against the disease, as per the national guidelines.
Today Health Minister Hon'ble Etela Rajender visited APHO of Rajeev Gandhi International Airport Shamshabad and observed universal screening.
