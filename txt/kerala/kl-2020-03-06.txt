Dedicated to People under Home isolation
In view of new cases being reported and community spread in Italy and Iran, Govt. of India has instructed to screen passengers coming through direct flights from Iran & Italy in addition to China, Hong Kong, Thailand, Singapore, Japan, South Korea, Vietnam, Nepal, Indonesia and Malaysia. All people coming from the affected areas of these countries and already notified countries should follow the advisory. People coming from Republic of Korea, Iran and Italy or having such travel history since 10th February 2020 may be quarantined for 14 days on arrival to India. Asymptomatic persons should remain in home isolation for 14 days. Symptomatic persons should contact the concerned designated isolation facilities.
Date:06/03/2020
WHO has declared the COVID-19 epidemic affecting 86 countries as a Public Health Emergency of International Concern. Due to the inflow of persons from affected countries, Kerala state has strengthened the surveillance and control measures against the disease. 592 Persons are placed under surveillance, out of which 546 are under home isolation and 46 are admitted in designated isolation facilities. Till date 631 samples of suspect cases have been sent to the National Institute of Virology for testing out of which results of 579 samples came as negative. The hospital authorities have confirmed that the health condition of all symptomatic persons under isolation in hospitals are stable.
In the background of the present COVID-19 outbreak, the general public is advised to adhere to health advisories issued by the State Government. Public should follow the cough etiquette by covering the nose and mouth using handkerchief/towel while sneezing/coughing. Do frequent hand washing with soap and water. Those who have arrived from the COVID-19affected countries should remain under strict home isolation as per the revised guidelines issued on 19/02/2020.
12 member high level team from Telengana visited Kerala to familiarise with the Corona prevention activities undertaken here. They visited the health minister and principal secretary, which was followed by meeting with various teams involved in the control activities. A brief presentation was made on the activities undertaken here. The team later visited the isolation facilities in Trivandrum.
