Appeal to Public
Any person with travel history to COVID19 affected countries or person with contact with such a traveler, whether they are having symptoms or not, must remain in home isolation. Avoid social greetings by touching, like hugs or handshake. Don't touch your face, nose and eyes. Use handkerchief while coughing/ sneezing. Use masks, only if you are taking a care of a person with suspected COVID-19 infection, or if you have symptoms. Healthy persons are not supposed to wear medical masks. Those who are wearing masks should know how to use and dispose it properly. N95 masks should not be worn by the public.
Date: 10/03/2020
WHO has declared the COVID-19 epidemic affecting 105 countries as a Public Health Emergency of International Concern. Due to the inflow of persons from affected countries, Kerala state has strengthened the surveillance and control measures against the disease. 1495 persons are placed under surveillance, out of which 1236 are under home isolation and 259 are admitted in designated isolation facilities. Till date 980 samples of suspect cases have been sent to the National Institute of Virology for testing out of which results of 815 samples came as negative. Testing facilities have been setup at Thiruvananthapuram and Kozhikode Medical Colleges also. Kozhikode Medical College has conducted test of 9 samples which were all negative. Thiruvananthapuram Medical college will start testing from Wednesday. In addition, permission has also been requested to start testing at Thrissur Medical College and Rajiv Gandhi Centre for Biotechnology.

The parents of the three year old child who was earlier tested positive have also been tested positive for COVID-19. 6 persons, 4 from Pathanamthitta and 2 from Kottayam who had contact with the positive cases from Pathanamthitta had earlier been declared positive for COVID-19. In the background of the present COVID-19 outbreak, the general public is advised to adhere to health advisories issued by the State Government. Public should follow the cough etiquette by covering the nose and mouth using handkerchief/towel while sneezing/coughing. Do frequent handwashing with soap and water. Those who have arrived from the COVID-19 affected countries should remain under strict home isolation.
