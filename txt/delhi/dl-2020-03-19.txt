4.	Following orders have been issued under epidemic regulation.
•	All educational institutions in NCT of Delhi Shall remain closed till 31st March 2020
•	All ongoing examination of schools/ colleges in NCT of Delhi shall be rescheduled after 31st March 2020
•	All restaurants in NCT of Delhi are directed to discontinue sitting arrangements in their premises till 31st March
•	All sports complexes in NCT of Delhi shall be closed till 31st March 2020
•	Any event ( Social,/Cultural/Political/Religious/Academic /sports/ Seminar and conferences ( Except Marriages) is restricted to maximum of 20 persons till 31st March 2020
•	All Private sector offices (including Multi National Compnies, IT firms Industries , Corporate offices etc.) situated in NCT of Delhi are advised to allow their offices . employees to work from home till 31st March 2020
•	General public is advised to remain at Home, particularly senior citizens, person affected with Hypertension/ Diabetes/ respiratory diseases/ Pregnant woman/cardiac diseases/ patient taking steroid and children.
