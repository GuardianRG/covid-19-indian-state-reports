1.	Following directions were issued under Delhi Epidemic Disease Act, COVID-19 Regulations.
•	The operations of all Gymnasium, SPA, Night Clubs and Theaters in NCT of Delhi to be suspended till 31st March, 2020.
•	Any gathering Social/ Cultural /Political /Religious/ Academic /Sports/ Seminar / Conference and Family mass gatherings ( except marriages) is restricted to maximum of 50 persons in NCT of Delhi till 31st March,2020
•	All Shopping Malls in NCT of Delhi should be disinfected daily and shall also make provisions of availability of ample number of hand sanitizers at main entrance and individual shops for visitors. All entry should be allowed only after cleaning of hands with hand sanitizers.
2.	Five quarantine facilities have been started for the persons returning from COVID-19 affected countries. All these facilities are linked with one Hospitals for providing logistics, sanitation staff, arranging for linen and its cleaning and Bio Medical waste Management. Total 427 passengers are under Quarantine at four facilities.
3.	Till date, 181872 passengers from COVID-19 affected countries have been screened at Delhi Airport and placed under surveillance. 4669 passengers from affected countries were screened today.
